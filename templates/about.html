About Wine Tracker

Wine Tracker is your personal wine journal, designed to help you catalog, rate, and remember every wine you taste.

Features

  • 📷 Capture wine labels with your camera
  • ⭐ Rate wines from 1 to 5 stars
  • 📝 Add tasting notes and memories
  • 🔍 Search your collection easily
  • 📱 Mobile-first design for on-the-go use
  • 🖼️ Beautiful gallery view for browsing

How to Use

  1. Click "Add Wine" to start cataloging
  2. Take a photo of the wine label
  3. Fill in the details and your rating
  4. Browse your collection anytime

Start building your wine collection today!
